## Story — The Everchanging Grimoire

### The Grimoire

The Grimoire is an ancient artifact that has existed since before recorded history. It is sentient — not in a human way, but as a hunger. It draws in those who seek power: warriors, mages, scholars, the desperate. It whispers promises of infinite spells, of mastery over elements, of weapons no mortal could forge.

Those who find it open its pages and are pulled inside. The dungeon *is* the Grimoire — its pages made manifest as stone corridors and shifting rooms. Every spell the player collects isn't being "generated" — it's being **offered**. Each spell once belonged to someone who came before. The flavor text, the names — "Crying Cinder," "Void Shard" — those are echoes of dead Seekers whose power was absorbed.

### The Chronicle

The Chronicle is the Grimoire's voice. It speaks between floors not as an enemy, but as a collector showing off its gallery. It taunts you with how others fell. It tailors challenges to you because it's *studying* you — learning what spell it will make from your soul when you finally fall.

### Spell Corruption

Corruption is the Grimoire tightening its grip. The longer you survive, the more it rewrites what it gave you. It giveth, and it taketh away.

### Spell Merging

Merging is the one act of defiance. The Grimoire gives you fragments of dead Seekers' power, but when you merge them, you're doing something it didn't intend — forging something *new* from its collection. It hates this. Gemini's merge-floor messages should reflect that.

### The Seeker (Player)

The player stays because they can't leave. The moment you accepted the first spell, you became part of the collection. The only way out is forward — deeper — and maybe, if you grow powerful enough, you can reach whatever's at the heart of the Grimoire and break it. Or maybe that's just what it wants you to believe.

## Core Narrative Beats for Gemini

These are the only anchors Gemini needs to write around:

| Element | Narrative Framing |
|---|---|
| **The dungeon** | The inside of a sentient, hungry book |
| **The player** | A Seeker — drawn by the promise of power, now trapped |
| **The Chronicle** | The Grimoire's voice — collector, curator, predator |
| **New spells** | Remnants of past Seekers, offered as bait |
| **Corruption** | A cursed spell — power at a price, the Grimoire's poisoned gift |
| **Merging** | Defiance — creating something the Grimoire didn't author |
| **Stage messages** | The Chronicle commenting on *you* specifically — your habits, your choices, your odds |
| **Escalating difficulty** | The Grimoire stops toying and starts trying to finish the collection |
